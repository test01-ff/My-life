Starwars is a popular science fiction franchise created by George Lucas. It began with the release of the original film, "Star Wars: Episode IV - A New Hope," in 1977. The franchise has since expanded to include multiple sequels, prequels, spin-offs, television series, books, comics, and video games. Star Wars is known for its epic space battles, iconic characters like Luke Skywalker, Darth Vader, and Princess Leia, and its exploration of themes such as good versus evil, the hero's journey, and the power of the Force.
